🎵 Music

  • :play - Play a song
  • :skip - Skip current song
  • :stop - Stop and clear queue
  • :pause - Pause music
  • :resume - Resume music
  • :queue - Show queue
  • :now - Current song
  • :loop - Loop song
  • :shuffle - Shuffle queue
  • :volume - Change volume
  • :remove - Remove from queue

📞 Voice

  • :join - Join your VC
  • :stay - Stay forever
  • :leave - Leave VC

🎮 Fun

  • :ping - Bot latency
  • :hello - Greeting
  • :roll - Roll dice
  • :flip - Flip coin
